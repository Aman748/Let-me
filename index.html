A New Online Delegated Purchasing Platform

Let Me

Let Me Do It is a online platform that created for those who need a helper to purchase and deliver items adhere to their requests. Those who is willing to pay to get the delegated purchasing service or we called as customer has to post his/her request on this platform and wait until there is a helper accepts the request. On the other side, whoever act as the "helper" has to accept the customer's request from this platform. Helper's job is to purchase items that ordered by customer and deliver them to the specific location that required by customer. After the job done, helper receive the service payment as his/her return. Let Me Do It has made a both benefited situation for the customer and the helper.
